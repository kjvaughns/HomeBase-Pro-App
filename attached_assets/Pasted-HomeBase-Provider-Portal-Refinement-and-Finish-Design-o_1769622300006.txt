HomeBase Provider Portal Refinement and Finish
Design only. React Native Expo. iOS-first. Liquid Glass. Calm luxury minimalism. No white outlined borders. Soft green only for primary actions and positive states. Everything clickable with mock data. Align UI patterns and data objects with the Homeowner portal so the experience feels like one product.

Primary goal
Finish the provider portal so it feels complete, connected, and effortless:
1. A real Client CRM with a premium client profile page and full job history.
2. A Provider Job Detail screen that mirrors what the homeowner sends through AI intake and booking.
3. A better Schedule tab with List-first, clear statuses, and a frictionless add job flow.
4. A stronger AI Service Builder and Services system that drives booking, schedule, and invoices.
5. A complete Business Profile and Public Booking Page editor with live preview and settings pages.
6. Add required business and money related pages: service areas, business info, bank, tax, reviews, resources, help center, contact support.
7. Make homeowner and provider experiences tightly connected with shared objects, shared terminology, and one tap actions.

Global requirements
1. Use the same design system as the homeowner app: typography scale, spacing, card style, chips, buttons, toasts, empty states, skeletons, and micro animations.
2. Fix safe area padding everywhere. No content hidden behind nav or FAB. FAB position consistent, never overlaps lists.
3. All lists are virtualized and feel fast. Add search and quick filters on every major list.
4. Provide realistic mock data with consistent relationships:
2 businesses
12 clients
30 jobs total across states (pending, upcoming, in progress, completed, invoiced, unpaid)
12 services (mixed pricing models)
Reviews dataset
HouseFax style home metadata per client
5. Update blueprint files as necessary for new routes, navigation, and data stubs.
