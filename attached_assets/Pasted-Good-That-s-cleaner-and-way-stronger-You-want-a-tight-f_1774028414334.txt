## 🔧 Prompt (Updated App Start Experience)

Redesign the HomeBase app launch and entry experience to be minimal, premium, and extremely fast.

## Launch / Splash Experience

When the app opens:

1. Show a clean splash screen

* Centered HomeBase logo
* Subtle fade or scale animation (0.8s–1.2s max)
* Background: clean, neutral (light or dark mode aware)
* No text, no loading spinners unless necessary

Then transition immediately to the entry screen.

---

## Entry Screen (Core Decision Screen)

After splash, show ONLY two options:

### Join as Homeowner

“Find and book trusted service pros”

### Join as Provider

“Run and grow your service business”

Design requirements:

* Two large card-style buttons or stacked buttons
* Strong visual hierarchy
* Plenty of spacing
* Smooth tap animations
* Use brand color as accent only (not overwhelming)
* No extra buttons, links, or distractions
* No navbar, no back button

This screen should feel like:
Apple-level simple and premium

---

## Interaction Behavior

When user taps:

### Homeowner

* Enter app in guest mode immediately
* No signup wall yet
* Let them explore

### Provider

* Immediately start onboarding flow
* No dashboard access until onboarding is complete

## Technical Requirements

* Splash screen should be native-feeling and fast
* Avoid long load times
* Preload minimal required assets only
* Smooth transition between splash → entry
